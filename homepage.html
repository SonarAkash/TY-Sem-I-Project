ABOUT

The Food Waste to Energy website is dedicated to transforming food waste into renewable energy through biogas conversion. By connecting restaurants, markets, and households with local biogas plants, we facilitate the efficient collection and management of food waste. Our platform provides valuable resources, educational content, and tools to track waste collection, promoting sustainability and community involvement. Join us in creating a cleaner, greener future by harnessing the potential of food waste!
